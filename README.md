# LF/HS Weekly Intelligence Brief — APJ

Weekly competitive intelligence for Life Fitness / Hammer Strength APJ field sales reps.

**Live:** https://trentraleigh89.github.io/Market-Intel-Brief-APJ/

## What this is

A web-based weekly briefing for APJ field reps, covering Tier 1 markets (Japan, China, South Korea, India, Australia, New Zealand, Singapore) and Tier 2 markets (Hong Kong, Taiwan, Malaysia, Thailand, Vietnam, Indonesia, Philippines). Same shell and editorial structure as the US and EMEA dashboards, with APJ-specific content sourced from native-language regional press where available.

- **Verticals:** Health Clubs, Hospitality, Multi-Unit Housing, Performance / Athletics, and Sport Science / Performance Education (same naming as EMEA; no Military vertical, which is US-only)
- **Tag vocabulary:** APJ-specific tags (JAPAN-CORPORATE-WELLNESS, ANZ-COMMERCIAL, CHINA-PREMIUM, KOREA-LUXURY, CRICKET, ASIAN-FOOTBALL, AFL-NRL, BRANDED-RESIDENCE, GATED-COMMUNITY) layered on the global taxonomy
- **Competitor coverage:** Global competitors prioritized by APJ relevance, with Matrix Fitness (Johnson Health Tech, Taiwan) and Impulse Health Tech (China) as Tier 1 APJ specialists alongside Technogym, plus Gym80 surfacing as a co-spec partner with Technogym at ANZ premium installations
- **Source priorities:** Native-language regional trade press where available (Australasian Leisure Management, TTG Asia, ESPNcricinfo, 证券时报 Securities Times China, measeoul-soofitnessspa.com Korea, VnExpress Vietnam, Spa+Clinic Australia, Hilton APAC newsroom) plus English-language regional and global outlets

## For reviewers

Same dashboard mechanics as US and EMEA:

- **Click any insight card** to flip and reveal the competitive implication and seller conversation starter
- **Filter chips** at the top of the brief: All, Trending, Saved, the five verticals, and Competitive (jumps to flags)
- **Bookmark icons** (top right of each card) save insights to the Saved view
- **Helpful row** at the top captures brief-level usefulness feedback
- **Region selector** in the hero (top right) — APJ will be added to the US and EMEA region selectors once content is approved by APJ leadership
- **Print button** generates a clean print view
- **Week picker** archives prior weeks once accumulated

Feedback goes to Trent.
